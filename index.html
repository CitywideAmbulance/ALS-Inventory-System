ALS Bag Restock

Scan items, enter quantities, submit

Instructions:
• First, select a bag number from the dropdown above the table.
• Scan or type a barcode/lot number/item name in the first column.
• If the item exists, the item name, lot, expiration, category, stock quantity, and status will be locked. Use Bag Qty to enter how many are going in the bag.
• The Inv Qty field shows the amount currently in stock when an item is found.
• If the item is not found, fill in the item fields and choose which required item it replaces, then click Add & Use.
• Once all rows are ready, click Submit All to record the bag contents.
