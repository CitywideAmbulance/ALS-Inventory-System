ALS Bag Restock

Scan items, enter quantities, submit

Instructions:
• First, select a bag number from the dropdown above the table.
• Scan or type a barcode/lot number/item name in the first column.
• If the item exists, fields auto‑fill; just enter the Bag Qty (how many go into the bag). Category and Status are read‑only.
• If the item is not found, an Inv Qty field appears. Enter the total quantity you are adding to stock, fill in all details, choose which required item it replaces, then click Add & Use. The item is saved to inventory, the Inv Qty field becomes disabled (so you can see what was added), and the status updates to reflect the item's expiration (OK / Expiring Soon / Expired). Category and Status remain read‑only. You can then adjust the Bag Qty before final submit.
• Once all rows are ready, click Submit All to record the bag contents.
